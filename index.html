about

Maya Watt is an artist living in London. Specializing in 3D modeling and digital drawing, she reflects on the moments of intimacy and loneliness in everyday life. She draws inspiration from analog video games, music, and physical phenomenal. She is drawn to tools that are playful and disarming in nature, i.e. iPads, coloured pencils, and construction paper.
